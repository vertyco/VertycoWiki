# Introduction
This Privacy Policy outlines how Vertyco ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our Discord server and related services. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

# Information We Collect
Vertyco does not directly collect or store personal financial information from users. All payment processing is handled by third-party payment processors including:
- PayPal
- Venmo
- CashApp
- Upgrade.Chat
- Stripe (via Upgrade.Chat)

# Discord Data
As a Discord community, we have access to:
- Your Discord username and discriminator
- Your Discord ID
- Your Discord avatar
- Messages you send in our server
- Your voice activity (only while you're connected to voice channels)
- Roles and permissions you hold within our server

# Game Data
For users who connect to our Ark Survival Evolved or Ark Survival Ascended servers, we collect:
- In-game character names
- Steam/Xbox/PlayStation IDs associated with your game account
- In-game activity logs
- IP addresses (used only for connection purposes and basic security)

# How We Use Information
We use the information we collect to:
- Maintain and improve our Discord server and game servers
- Process donations and assign appropriate donor roles and benefits
- Enforce our community rules
- Track VertCoin (VC) balances and transactions
- Communicate with users about their accounts, donations, or support requests

# Information Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our servers, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

# Data Retention
- Discord-related data is retained as long as you remain a member of our server
- Game server data may be retained for troubleshooting and rule enforcement purposes
- Virtual currency (VC) transaction records are retained indefinitely for accounting and dispute resolution purposes

# Third-Party Services
Payment processing is handled by third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:
- PayPal: https://www.paypal.com/us/webapps/mpp/ua/privacy-full
- Stripe: https://stripe.com/privacy
- Venmo: https://venmo.com/legal/us-privacy-policy/
- CashApp: https://cash.app/legal/us/en-us/privacy
- Upgrade.Chat: https://docs.upgrade.chat/privacy-policy

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

# Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we have about you
- Request deletion of your data from our systems
- Opt out of communications
To exercise these rights, please contact us through our Discord support ticket system.

# Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in our Discord server's announcements channel.
