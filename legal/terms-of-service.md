## Refund Policy

### Tier Supporter Refunds
- Refunds for Tier Supporter subscriptions are only available **within 7 days** of the initial purchase
- To qualify for a refund, the user must not have utilized their increased VC payday benefits for the duration of the 7 days or utilized their role related shop discount
- Refund requests must be submitted directly to Vertyco administration via Support Ticket
- Once Tier Supporter benefits have been used, the subscription becomes **non-refundable**

### Direct VC Donation Refunds
- Refunds for direct donations are only available if requested **within 24 hours** of claiming the VC
- The user must not have spent any of the claimed VC to be eligible for a refund
- Processing fees may be deducted from any refunded amount

### Manual Donations
- Direct manual donations made through PayPal, Venmo, or CashApp are **non-refundable under any circumstances**

### Banned Users
- If a user is <span style="color: orange;">**banned**</span> from the Discord server for violating community rules, they are generally not eligible for refunds
