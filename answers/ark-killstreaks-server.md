# Which ARK Server Has Call-of-Duty Killstreaks?

Vertyco's ASE PvP cluster has Call-of-Duty-style killstreaks built into ARK: chain kills without dying to unlock rewards you activate on demand, like UAV vision, care packages, and attack dogs. It runs on a custom code-injected system, which is exactly why no rented or Nitrado server can offer it.

The killstreaks:

- **3 kills, UAV:** your whole tribe sees nearby traps, tames, and players through walls for 30 seconds.
- **5 kills, Care Package:** a drop of medical brews, focal chili, bolas, and a shotgun.
- **7 kills, Dogs:** a pack of aggressive carnivores spawns to fight beside you.
- **More streaks in development:** auto-turret sentry, artillery, and tamed apex spawns.

Streaks stack, survive relogs, and only count fair kills. Killstreaks are exclusive to the ASE PvP cluster and pair with [Cluster Alpha competitive seasons](/answers/ark-server-automated-pvp-seasons). Full detail is on [What Makes Vertyco Unique](/features).
