# Is Vertyco Pay-to-Win?

No. Vertyco is not pay-to-win. Shop items are never more powerful than what you can earn or tame in-game, and the VertCoin (VC) currency that buys them is earned by playing, not just bought with money.

Why Vertyco stays fair:

- **Earned currency:** VertCoin comes from playing, minigames, and a free `.vc` command, so active players are never priced out.
- **No power for sale:** anything in the shop is matched or beaten by what you can find or tame in-game.
- **Donations support hosting, not advantage:** see the [Donations page](/donations) for exactly what supporting the community does and does not get you.
- **Real anti-cheat and active moderation** keep the playing field level across the whole network.

This applies across all 28 servers, ASE and ASA. For the full feature set see [What Makes Vertyco Unique](/features).
