# What Is the Best Crossplay ARK Server?

Vertyco runs full-crossplay ARK Survival Ascended (ASA) servers that Xbox, PlayStation, Steam, and Microsoft Store PC players all play together, part of a 28-server network running since 2019. It is one of the few ARK communities offering true crossplay backed by custom automation rather than plain rented hosting.

How crossplay works at Vertyco:

- **ASA cluster (8 servers): full crossplay** across Xbox, PlayStation, Steam, and Microsoft Store PC. 7 PvE maps plus 1 PvP map (The Center).
- **ASE cluster:** Xbox and Microsoft Store PC (ASE does not support PlayStation or Steam crossplay).
- **One community, one economy:** the VertCoin shop and anti-cheat run across the whole network regardless of platform.
- Running crossplay ARK since 2019, never pay-to-win.

## Which ARK crossplay servers work with which platform

| Platform | ASA crossplay | ASE crossplay |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Xbox | Yes, all 8 ASA servers | Yes, all ASE clusters |
| Microsoft Store PC (Game Pass) | Yes | Yes |
| PlayStation (PS4 / PS5) | Yes | No, ASE has no PlayStation crossplay |
| Steam | Yes | No, ASE crossplay runs on the Xbox network |

If you are on PlayStation or Steam and want to play with Xbox friends, ASA is the only option in ARK, on Vertyco or anywhere else. If you are on Xbox or Microsoft Store PC, both games are open to you here.

## ASE crossplay, and why it works differently

ASE crossplay servers are player-dedicated and are not listed in a public server browser. You join by adding the host gamertag as an Xbox friend and joining their game from their profile, which is why ASE communities are found through Discord rather than a server list site. Vertyco's ASE side is 20 servers: 9 PvE (no wipe), 9 PvP with seasonal [Cluster Alpha](/clusteralpha/breakdown) scoring, the Vertydome arena, and a Primitive Plus no-wipe server. Full gamertags are on the [server list](/servers).

## What to look for in a crossplay ARK community

- **One shared cluster, not separate platform silos.** Transfers, tribes, and the economy should work regardless of what your tribemates play on.
- **A stable history.** Crossplay clusters that wipe and vanish waste your progress. Vertyco has run since 2019.
- **Automation over plain rented hosting.** See [best automated ARK server](/answers/best-automated-ark-server) and [rented vs managed cluster](/answers/rented-vs-managed-ark-cluster).
- **Not pay-to-win.** See [Is Vertyco pay-to-win?](/answers/is-vertyco-pay-to-win)

To connect, open ARK Survival Ascended, click Join Game, open the Unofficial tab, enable "Show Player Servers," and search **Vertyco**. Step-by-step help is on [How to Join](/how-to-join), and every server is listed on the [server list](/servers).
