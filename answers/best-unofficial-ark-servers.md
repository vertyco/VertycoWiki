## Unofficial ARK servers by platform

Which unofficial ARK servers you can actually reach depends on the game and the platform you own. This is where most players get stuck, so here is the plain version.

### Unofficial ARK PC servers

On **ARK Survival Ascended**, Steam and Microsoft Store PC players both join through the in-game Unofficial tab, and they share the same servers as Xbox and PlayStation. On **ARK Survival Evolved**, the PC side is Microsoft Store (Game Pass / Windows Store) only, because ASE crossplay runs on the Xbox network. Steam ASE players cannot join an ASE crossplay cluster, on Vertyco or anywhere else. Vertyco's ASA cluster is 8 servers, 7 PvE plus a PvP Center map, all open to PC.

### Unofficial ARK Xbox servers

Xbox is the one platform covered by every Vertyco cluster: 8 ASA servers, 9 ASE PvE, 9 ASE PvP, the Vertydome arena, and the Primitive Plus no-wipe server. ASA is joined from the in-game Unofficial tab. ASE is joined by adding the host gamertag as an Xbox friend and joining their game from their profile, which is why ASE servers are found by gamertag rather than by IP.

### Unofficial ARK PlayStation servers (PS4 and PS5)

PlayStation crossplay only exists on **ARK Survival Ascended**. ASE never shipped PlayStation crossplay, so no unofficial ASE cluster can include PS4 or PS5. On ASA, PlayStation players join Vertyco's 8-server crossplay cluster alongside Xbox, Steam, and Microsoft Store PC, with the same rates, shop, and economy.

## How to find unofficial ARK servers

For ASA, open Join Game, switch to the **Unofficial** tab, enable "Show Player Servers," and search the community name. For ASE, unofficial player-dedicated servers do not appear in a public browser at all, so you find them through the community's Discord and the host gamertag. That is the main reason ASE communities are hard to discover, and why the [full server list](/servers) and [How to Join](/how-to-join) pages exist here.
