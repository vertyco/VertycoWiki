## 👑 Cluster Alpha - Automated Competitive PvP (ASE PvP)

Cluster Alpha turns our **ASE PvP cluster** into a season-long competition that runs itself, with **no admin bias and no favoritism.** Every tribe earns a live Power rating from what they actually build, and an Alpha Points score earned purely through PvP.

- **Zero-sum economy:** points are stolen, never handed out. Kill an enemy or raid their base and their points transfer to you.
- **Kills pay out:** rewards scale with the victim's power, with underdog bonuses for punching up, bounties on the top tribes, and "most wanted" bonuses for hunting the top killers.
- **Killstreak medals, raiding, rivalries,** and automatic daily and weekly recaps of the standings.
- **Season rewards:** the top 3 tribes get VertCoin payouts before each wipe, and 1st place earns a starter pack for next season.

No other ARK community automatically crowns its best tribe from real data. See the full [Cluster Alpha Breakdown](/clusteralpha/breakdown) and [worked Scenarios](/clusteralpha/scenarios).

## 🔪 CoD-Style Killstreaks (ASE PvP)

Killstreaks like Call of Duty, in ARK. Chain kills in a row and unlock rewards you activate on demand. This runs on a custom system, which is exactly why no rented or Nitrado server can offer it.

- **3 kills, UAV:** your whole tribe sees nearby traps, tames, and players through walls for 30 seconds.
- **5 kills, Care Package:** a drop of medical brews, focal chili, bolas, and a shotgun.
- **7 kills, Dogs:** a pack of aggressive carnivores spawns to fight beside you.
- **More streaks in development:** auto-turret sentry, artillery, and tamed apex spawns.

Streaks stack, survive relogs, and only count fair kills.

## ⏰ Smart Offline-Raid Protection (ASE PvP)

Vanilla ARK offline raid protection is broken. Ours is custom built and fair to both sides. After your tribe goes offline, your turrets deal bonus damage to raiders, so you are not free loot while you sleep.

- **Schedule-aware:** active through the work week and always on during major holidays.
- **Anti-stall, both ways:** raiders can hold it off by staying active, but a short break lets it kick in. Defenders can check their status in-game before logging off.
- **Transparent:** a single in-game command tells you whether a base is protected and when its protection activates.
