# üìã General Discord Rules & Procedures (2025 Update)

> **Important Philosophy Change:**
> To keep the community active and welcoming, we are moving away from permanent bans for most Discord offenses. Instead, we will use temporary bans, timeouts, and warnings as our primary punishments. **Permanent bans are now reserved for only the most severe, real-world violations.**

## üö´ Offenses That Still Result in Permanent Ban

- Filing a chargeback
- DDoS attacks or threats
- Doxxing (releasing private/personal information)
- Real-world threats of violence or harm (e.g., "I know where you live")

All other offenses will result in temporary bans, timeouts, wipes, or warnings as described below.

## 1Ô∏è‚É£ General Rules

### 1.1 - Disruptiveness/Toxicity/Hate Speech

#### Disruptiveness/Toxicity
- **First Offense**: Warning issued to the user
- **Second Offense**: Timeout issued (1-3 days)
- **Third Offense**: Lengthier timeout issued (up to 2 weeks)
- **Fourth Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">**7-day temp ban**</span> issued to the user

#### Hate Speech & Slurs
- **First Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">**7-day temp ban**</span>
- **Second Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">**15-day temp ban**</span>
- **Third Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">**60-day temp ban**</span>
- **Real-world threats, doxxing, or similar**: <span style="color: orange;">**Permanent ban**</span>

Examples include:
- Racial slurs
- Targeted harassment in DMs
- Real-world threats (permaban)

### 1.2 - Staff Contact Policy
*Do not privately message staff, use the support ticket system to get help faster.*
- **First Offense**: Simple verbal warning, no record kept
- **Second Offense**: Formal warning may be issued
- **Third Offense**: Timeout may be issued
- **Fourth Offense**: Lengthier timeout may be issued
- **Fifth Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">**7-day temp ban**</span> may be issued depending on circumstances

### 1.3 - Privacy Protection *(Zero Tolerance)*
*No revealing private information about any individual.*
- **First Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">**Permanent ban**</span> issued to the user

### 1.4 - Accusation Policy
*Do not publicly accuse other users/players of misconduct. Use a support ticket.*
- **First Offense**: Warning issued to the user
- **Second Offense**: Timeout issued (1-3 days)
- **Third Offense**: Lengthier timeout issued (up to 2 weeks)
- **Fourth Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">**7-day temp ban**</span> issued to the user

### 1.5 - Prohibited Content
*No posting content related to piracy, cheats, cracks, exploits, or NSFW material.*
- **First Offense**: Warning issued to the user
- **Second Offense**: Timeout issued (1-3 days)
- **Third Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">**14-day temp ban**</span> issued to the user

### 1.6 - Begging Policy
*Do not beg other players/admins for items or virtual currency (VC).*
- **First Offense**: Warning issued to the user
- **Second Offense**: Timeout issued (1-3 days)
- **Third Offense**: Lengthier timeout issued (up to 2 weeks)
- **Fourth Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">**7-day temp ban**</span> issued to the user

### 1.7 - Bug/Misuse Policy
*Items/Dinos lost due to bugs or misuse of the shop cannot be reimbursed.*
> This is a general policy with no punishment tiers.

### 1.8 - Dating Service Policy
*This is not a dating service; DM flirt attempts will lead to removal.*
- **First Offense**: Warning issued to the user
- **Second Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">**Ban**</span> issued to the user

## 2Ô∏è‚É£ Account Rules

### 2.1 - Multiple Accounts Policy
*Multiple Discord/Player accounts are not permitted.*
- **First Offense**: 
    - <span style="color: orange;">**Ban**</span> the alt account
    - Formal warning for the main account
- **Second Offense**: 
    - <span style="color: orange;">**7-day temp ban**</span> for both the main account and permanently ban the alt

> If the player is in a tribe by themselves when they get <span style="color: orange;">**banned**</span>, the tribe will be wiped.

### 2.2 - Alt Account Harboring
*Harboring alt accounts for other people is not allowed.*
- **First Offense**:
    - Recruiter receives formal warning
    - Knowing participants receive warning
    - No punishment for uninvolved tribe members
- **Second Offense**:
    - Recruiter receives Ark data wipe
    - Knowing participants receive formal warning
    - No punishment for uninvolved tribe members
- **Third Offense**:
    - Recruiter receives <span style="color: orange;">30-day temp ban</span>
    - Knowing participants receive Ark data wipe
    - No punishment for uninvolved tribe members

> Players without Discord have 24 hours to join and link their account or face a <span style="color: orange;">**ban**</span>.

### 2.3 - Username/Avatar Policy
*Overly offensive/super NSFW usernames or avatars are not allowed.*

#### NSFW Avatar Guidelines
- Simple bikini/underwear/thirst trap photos are acceptable
- Actual nudity is prohibited
- **First Offense**: 24 hours to change or be <span style="color: orange;">**14-day temp banned**</span>

#### Extremist Content
- Anything promoting hate speech, violence, or generally offensive content
- **First Offense**: 24 hours to change or be <span style="color: orange;">**14-day temp banned**</span>
- Racial slurs result in <span style="color: orange;">14-day temp ban</span>

### 2.4 - Economic Activity Policy *(Zero Tolerance)*
*Conducting any real-world economic activity on this server is strictly prohibited.*
- **First Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">**Ban**</span> issued to the user

### 2.5 - Role Recovery Policy
*Any roles lost from leaving will not be returned upon rejoining.*
> This is a general policy with no punishment tiers.

### 2.6 - Ban Appeal Rules
#### Prohibited Actions:
- Do NOT ask when someone's ban appeal will be approved
- It is ‚úÖ **acceptable** to ask if someone was <span style="color: orange;">**banned**</span>.
- Staff may explain <span style="color: orange;">**ban**</span> reasons to other users.

#### Ban Appeal Begging Penalties:
- **First Offense**: Verbal reminder to use proper channels.
- **Second Offense**: Formal warning issued via `.warn`.
- **Third Offense**: VC balance wipe and 12-hour timeout.
- **Fourth Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">14-day temp ban</span> issued to the user.

> All appeals must go through the ban appeal Discord: https://discord.gg/KZFbS4aJxA

### 2.7 - Staff Impersonation *(Zero Tolerance)*
*Impersonating admins or staff results in a ban.*
- **First Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">Permanent ban</span> issued to the user

> - ‚úÖ The alt account has not been used in the server (no registration or VC claims).
> - ‚úÖ The alt immediately contacts Staff about their appeal.
> - ‚úÖ The alt was not in the server simultaneously with the main account.

### Appeal Process:
1. Use the `.appeal` command and provide:
    - Main account's ID and <span style="color: orange;">**ban**</span> reason.
