> **Our Philosophy:**
> PvE is the family-friendly side of our community. We enforce strict rules to ensure a welcoming environment for all players. Antisocial behavior is not tolerated.

- **1.1 - General Discord Rules**
  - *General Discord rules from the main rules channel also apply.* This is a general rule with no specific punishment tiers.

- **1.2 - Game Bug Policy**
  - *Items or tames lost due to game bugs cannot be replaced by staff.* This is a general rule with no specific punishment tiers.

- **1.3 - Community Conduct**
  - *Be considerate and friendly to other players.*

  - **Second Offense**: 3-day timeout from Discord.
  - **Third Offense**: Base wipe may be issued.
  - **Fourth Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">Ban</span> may be issued.

- **1.4 - Admin Structures**
  - *Do not demolish Admin structures.*
  - **First Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">Ban</span> issued to the player.

## 2Ô∏è‚É£ Tribe Rules

- **2.1 - Tribe at Your Own Risk**
  - *Staff cannot assist with internal tribe conflicts.* This is a general rule with no specific punishment tiers.
- **2.2 - Tribe-wide Enforcement Policy**
  - *Punishments affect the entire tribe, regardless of individual involvement. Members should carefully consider who they tribe with.*
- **2.3 - Staff Non-Intervention Policy**
  - *Staff does not resolve internal tribe affairs, including drama, merges, and tame ownership disputes.*

## 3Ô∏è‚É£ Good Neighbor Rules

- **3.1 - No Caging Players**
  - **First Offense**: 7-day <span style="color: orange;">Temp Ban</span>.
  - **Second Offense**: 30-day <span style="color: orange;">Temp Ban</span>.
  - **Third Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">Permanent Ban</span>.
- **3.2 - No Stealing**
  - **First Offense**: 30-day <span style="color: orange;">Temp Ban</span>.
  - **Second Offense**: 90-day <span style="color: orange;">Temp Ban</span>.
  - **Third Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">Permanent Ban</span>.
- **3.3 - No Passive Killing**

  - **First Offense**: 30-day <span style="color: orange;">Temp Ban</span>.
  - **Second Offense**: 90-day <span style="color: orange;">Temp Ban</span>.
  - **Third Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">Permanent Ban</span>.
- **3.4 - No Interference**
  - *Do not interfere with players taming wild dinos or OSD activities on Extinction.*
  - **First Offense**: Warning.
  - **Second Offense**: 7-day <span style="color: orange;">Temp Ban</span>.
  - **Third Offense**: 30-day <span style="color: orange;">Temp Ban</span>.
  - **Fourth Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">Permanent Ban</span>.

## 4Ô∏è‚É£ Structure Rules

- **4.1 - Base Limitations**
  - *One main base and one outpost (< 300 structures) are allowed. Only one location can be in a cave.*
  - **First Offense**: 48 hours to comply or face base removal.
  - **Second Offense**: Immediate base wipe.
  - **Third Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">Ban</span>.
- **4.2 - Size Restrictions**
  - *Bases must fit within render distance (80 foundations maximum).*
  - **First Offense**: 48 hours to comply.
  - **Second Offense**: Partial structure removal.
  - **Third Offense**: Complete base wipe.
- **4.3 - Beach Building**
  - *No established bases on beaches.*
  - **First Offense**: 24 hours to relocate.
  - **Second Offense**: Immediate structure removal.
- **4.4 - Blocking Restricted Areas**
  - *Do not block spawns, rare resources, obelisks, or Extinction terminals.*
  - **First Offense**: 24 hours to remove structures.
  - **Second Offense**: Immediate structure removal.
  - **Third Offense**: Complete base wipe.
- **4.5 - Artifact Cave Building**
  - *Artifacts and Explorer Notes must remain accessible to all players.*
  - **First Offense**: 48 hours to provide access.
  - **Second Offense**: Structure removal.
- **4.6 - Teleporter Limit**
  - *A maximum of two teleporters are allowed per tribe.*
  - **First Offense**: 24 hours to remove excess teleporters.
  - **Second Offense**: Admin removal of all teleporters.
- **4.7 - Taming Trap Cleanup**
  - **First Offense**: Warning.
  - **Second Offense**: Admin removal of the trap.
  - **Third Offense**: Base wipe.
- **4.8 - No Meshing**
  - **First Offense**: Structure removal.
  - **Second Offense**: Base wipe.
  - **Third Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">Ban</span>.
- **4.9 - Territory Trading**
  - *Do not sell territory for VC or block areas for trade.*
  - **First Offense**: Warning and structure removal.
  - **Second Offense**: Base wipe.
  - **Third Offense**: <span style="color: orange;">Ban</span>.

## 5Ô∏è‚É£ Tame Rules

- **5.1 - Mass Breeding**
  - *Do not leave dinos breeding while unattended.*
  - **First Offense**: Warning and notification to address the issue.
  - **Second Offense**: Removal of excess babies after a warning.
  - **Third Offense**: Removal of all breeding pairs.
  - **Fourth Offense**: Removal of all tames.
- **5.2 - Tame Limits**
  - *A maximum of 50 tames are allowed outside of cryopods.*
  - **First Offense**: 24 hours to comply.
  - **Second Offense**: Admin removal of excess tames.
  - **Third Offense**: Removal of all tames.
- **5.3 - Abandoned Tames**
  - *Do not leave tames at obelisks or in public areas.*
  - **First Offense**: Admin removal without warning.
- **5.4 - Tame Containment**
  - *All tames must be kept within your base limits.*
  - **First Offense**: Warning.
  - **Second Offense**: Admin removal of wandering tames.
  - **Third Offense**: Removal of all tames.

## üìñ Definitions

> For a complete list of terms, see the [Glossary](/glossary).

- **Outpost**: A secondary base with fewer than 300 structures.
- **Main Base**: Your primary base of operations.
- **Meshing**: Placing a structure under the map's texture in a way that makes it hard or impossible to destroy.
  - A structure is considered meshed when **any** of the following conditions are met:
    - Cannot be damaged by conventional means (C4, Rockets, Tek Rifle). Tames do not count.
    - Is fully under the texture of the map (fully hidden from normal view)
- **VC**: VertCoin, the Discord server's virtual currency.
